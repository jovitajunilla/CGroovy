ABOUT US

The History

Our history began in 2021 with a small group of passionate music enthusiasts who wanted to change the way people experience music. Over the years, we've grown into a full-fledged music streaming platform with thousands of songs, artists, and listeners from around the world.

Journey Progression

2021 - The Beginning
C'Groovy was founded by a small group of music enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform for independent artists to share their music freely

2022 - Product Launch
We launched our first version of the mobile and web app, offering basic streaming features and a library focused on indie and local talent.

2023 - Growing the Platform
C'Groovy expanded its catalog by partnering with international indie labels. We introduced AI-powered music recommendations and user-generated playlists.

2024 - Community $ Innovation
We launched features like artist live sessions, social sharing tools, and collaborative playlists. Our user base grew significantly through community-driven events.

2025 - Redefining the Experience
C'Groovy introduced social listening rooms, real-time lyrics, and subscription-based premium features. We also began investing in exclusive content and original artist programs.
